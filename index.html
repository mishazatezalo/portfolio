Projects

LionLink

Why?

LionLink was created to address a significant problem many students face on large university campuses: loneliness and difficulty forming connections in a sea of over 50,000 people. With so many students and events happening simultaneously, it can be overwhelming and isolating for students to find a sense of belonging. LionLink’s goal is to provide a safe, engaging platform where students can discover events, meet new people, and form meaningful connections. By focusing on creating a digital space that fosters community, LionLink aims to solve the problem of campus isolation and encourage social engagement.

UX/UI Design

From a UI/UX design perspective, the name LionLink was chosen to reflect the university’s mascot and symbolize connection between students. The color scheme was crafted with soft yet energetic tones—blending school pride with a welcoming atmosphere. The layout was designed to be simple and user-friendly, ensuring that students can easily browse events, connect with peers, and explore campus activities without feeling overwhelmed. A consistent visual hierarchy, with clear calls to action and smooth navigation, ensures that users can intuitively find what they’re looking for, creating a positive, engaging user experience.

Full-Stack Implementation

On the full-stack engineering side, I used React for the front-end and Node.js with MongoDB for the back-end to create a scalable platform that could handle hundreds of users concurrently. The platform features secure user authentication and real-time updates for events, allowing students to interact with live data and stay up-to-date with campus activities. I also implemented a web scraper to automate event data collection, reducing manual input by 80%. The seamless integration between the front-end and back-end ensured fast load times, smooth interactions, and a reliable platform, allowing LionLink to deliver on its promise of fostering connection in a large university setting.

Future Improvements

Currently, I’m working on revamping the design of LionLink to enhance both functionality and aesthetics. I’m focusing on improving the overall user experience by streamlining navigation, updating the color palette to create a more modern and engaging feel, and optimizing the layout for better accessibility across all devices. Additionally, I’m integrating user feedback to refine features, making the platform even more intuitive and responsive. This redesign aims to make LionLink a more immersive and effective tool for students to connect and engage with their campus community.
